# Quarterly Learning Performance Summary (Synthetic)

> Synthetic aggregate report for demonstration only.

## Headline Metrics

- Average study time across learners: 21 hours.
- Overall certification pass rate: 68%.

## Key Observations

- Learners with more than 20 study hours AND more than 75% on practice scores
  show substantially stronger certification outcomes.
- Candidates who completed 3 or more practice exams passed at roughly 90%.
- Morning studiers scored on average ~8% higher than evening studiers on the
  same certification track.

## Weak-Area Trends

- Storage is the most common weak area for AZ-204 candidates.
- Stream Analytics is the most common weak area for DP-203 candidates.
- CI/CD and GitHub Actions recur as weak areas for AZ-400 candidates.

## Recommendation

Encourage early practice exams and protect morning focus blocks for study.
Group weak-area workshops (e.g. an AZ-204 Storage clinic) lift whole-team scores.
